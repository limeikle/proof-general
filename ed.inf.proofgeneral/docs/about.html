Proof General Eclipse

Developed at University of Edinburgh by Daniel Winterstein, Alex Heneveld and David Aspinall, 2005-7.

Proof General Eclipse is a re-implementation of the Emacs based Proof General.

To find out more about Proof General Eclipse, see http://proofgeneral.inf.ed.ac.uk/wiki/ProofGeneralEclipse.
